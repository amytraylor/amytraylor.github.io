Basic Shapes

A simple introduction to drawing with p5.js.

Try this:

  • Click anywhere on the canvas to add a circle
  • Press any key to clear and regenerate

This sketch demonstrates:

  • The coordinate system (0,0 is top-left)
  • Basic shapes: ellipse(), rect(), line()
  • Color with fill() and stroke()
  • Randomness with random()
